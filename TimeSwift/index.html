What is TimeSwift?


TimeSwift is a website based around solving your timekeeping needs while being fast and private. You can see how we achieved this goal below. We've made TimeSwift as simple and easy to use as possible so you can swiftly access the information you need. There's lots to do on our website like timers, stopwatches, holiday countdowns, world clocks, local clocks, and more to come in the future

Why TimeSwift?


Private

TimeSwift respects your privacy. We don't use any cookies or invasive trackers and the analytics tool we use (Matomo On-Premise) is ran on the host servers so no data is shared with Matomo or Google, and you can even opt out on TimeSwift's help page.

Fast and simple

TimeSwift streamlines your timekeeping needs. Because we don't use any ads or analytics tools that have to load in lots of external trackers, TimeSwift is fast and the simple layout with darker colours is simple to navigate and easier on the eyes.

Helpful

TimeSwift has an extensive list of helpful pages on our website for all your timekeeping needs like timers, a stopwatch, holiday countdowns, weather info, the time and date and a world clock with more being added all the time.
